Discover Our Exclusive Chalets!

Enjoy a unique mountain retreat with our exclusive chalets, available only for Maunga Club members.

Chalet Information

  • Only Club members can access the chalets.
  • All chalet bookings must be from Friday to Sunday.
  • Chalets offer shared accommodation for between 6 and 40 people per chalet.
  • All three chalets are on the mountain, allowing you to ski to and from the chalets.
  • The mountain provides 550 hectares of skiing/snowboarding area, and the longest ski/snowboard season in New Zealand.
