JOIN MAUNGA CLUB

Maunga Club offers exciting membership benefits. To join, follow these steps:

  1. Email your full name, physical address, proof of address, and proof of ID to membership@clubmaunga.com.
  2. We will review your application and get in touch with further instructions.
  3. Once your application is approved, you'll become an official Maunga Club member!

Membership Benefits:

  • Access to exclusive chalets for skiing and snowboarding.
  • Discounted rates on cabin rentals.
  • Special events and activities for members.

Contact Us:

If you have any questions about the joining process, feel free to contact us at info@clubmaunga.com.
